# Career Accelerator Pre-Work — Participant Instructions

*This template is used across Career Accelerator sessions. Facilitators: customize the "Session-specific" section below for each event.*

---

## Before You Arrive

Thank you for signing up for this Career Accelerator session. To get the most out of our time together, please complete the following before the session.

**Time required:** approximately 15–20 minutes

### All sessions: reflect on these questions

1. What brought you to business analysis? (the real answer, not the polished one)
2. Where do you feel most confident in your BA practice right now?
3. What's one thing you're working on that feels stuck or unclear?

You don't need to write formal answers — a few notes to yourself is enough. We'll build on this in the session.

### Session-specific preparation

*[Facilitator: replace this section with the specific pre-work for your session type. Examples:*

*— **Career Mapping:** Review the BABOK competency framework overview (linked below). Rate yourself 1–5 on each area before arriving.*

*— **Resume Lab:** Bring your current resume (or a draft). Also bring one job posting you'd genuinely apply to.*

*— **Mock Interview:** Think of 3 real projects you can speak to in detail. You'll use these for practice.*

*— **Case Workshop:** No materials needed. Come ready to think out loud.*

*— **Skills Not Titles Panel:** Write down 2 roles outside "Business Analyst" you've been curious about.]*

---

## What to Expect

Career Accelerator sessions are hands-on and conversational. You'll be asked to:
- Share your perspective (no wrong answers)
- Work in pairs or small groups
- Receive and give structured feedback

You don't need to have everything figured out. The point is to make progress together.

## Questions?

Reach out to the ETN program coordinator before the session if you have questions.
