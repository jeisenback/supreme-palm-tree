## Notes for Bio Collection

When requesting bios from panelists, send this prompt:

> "We'd love a short bio (100–150 words) for the event registration page. Feel free to include your current role, relevant experience, and anything that gives attendees a sense of your perspective. If you have a headshot you'd like us to use, please attach a JPG (at least 400×400px).
>
> Alternatively, if you'd prefer we draft something from your LinkedIn for your review, just let us know."

**Deadline:** Request bios at least 2 weeks before the event. Follow up at 1 week if not received.
