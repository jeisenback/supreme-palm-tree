About TrailBlaze Adventures
TrailBlaze Adventures is a mid-sized outdoor adventure travel company operating in the eastern US. TrailBlaze offers guided trips ranging from half-day hikes to week-long expeditions, primarily serving urban professionals and small family groups looking for weekend outdoor experiences. The company employs 12 full-time staff and works with approximately 40 contracted guides.

The situation
Weekend trip bookings have declined 18% over the past two quarters. Leadership has noticed but does not agree on why.

- The CEO believes the company needs a new booking platform to stay competitive.
- The Head of Operations believes the real issue is guide availability — too many requests are being turned away.
- The Marketing Lead believes the brand is invisible to the target demographic online.
- The Finance Director has frozen the technology budget until a business case is presented.

No business analyst has been involved yet. Leadership is already moving toward a solution.

Key stakeholders
- CEO
- Head of Operations
