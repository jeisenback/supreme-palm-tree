TrailBlaze Personas

--- DOCUMENT GUIDE ---
What this is:   Pre-written customer and stakeholder personas for the TrailBlaze case study.
Who it's for:   Participants and facilitator.
When to use it: Distribute with Session 1 materials. Participants use these as context during exercises and as a model for the optional Session 1 homework persona exercise. Persona creation is the homework task, not an in-session exercise.
--- END GUIDE ---

1) Weekend Warrior — "Alex"
- Age: 29, Occupation: Marketing Specialist
- Needs: Quick, well‑planned weekend getaways; easy booking; mobile itinerary; low setup time
- Pain points: Limited weekend time; travel planning friction; uncertainty around guide quality
- Acceptance signals: completes booking in <5 minutes; downloads itinerary; NPS ≥8 after trip

2) Family Planner — "Maria"
- Age: 42, Occupation: School Administrator
- Needs: Safe, family‑friendly options, clear packing list, reliable logistics
- Pain points: coordinating kids and schedules, concerns about safety/medical needs
- Acceptance signals: family booking completed; clear pre‑trip checklist received; 0 safety incidents

3) Lead Guide — "Sam"
- Age: 36, Occupation: Professional Guide
- Needs: Clear assignment, itinerary, roster, route maps, emergency contacts
- Pain points: last‑minute changes, unclear responsibilities, payment delays
- Acceptance signals: acknowledged assignment within 12 hours; compensation on time
