Lead Guide Representative	High	Medium	Monthly feedback, included in pilot planning	Operational feasibility input
Marketing Lead	Medium	Medium	Weekly marketing sync, provide promo assets	Pilot promotion budget approval
Customers	High	Low	Surveys, pilot communications	Provide acceptance feedback
Finance Director	Low	High	Budget reviews, milestone signoffs	Approves pilot costs and pricing
Legal/Compliance	Low	Medium	Policy review for permits/data	Approves contract & permit language
