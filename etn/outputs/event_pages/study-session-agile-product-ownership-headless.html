Study Session - Agile and Product Ownership Analysis

Oct 14, 2025 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time  

Dive into the Agile Extension and Product Ownership Analysis (POA) Guide. Learn how business analysis empowers product teams to deliver value in adaptive environments.

Date: Tuesday, October 14th
Time: 7:00 PM EST
Location: Zoom

Led by: David Thomson, CBAP®, AAC, CPOA

Description:
Agile and product-focused work require a unique mindset and toolset. This session introduces the Agile Extension to the BABOK® Guide and the Guide to Product Ownership Analysis (POA), highlighting how business analysis fits within adaptive frameworks. We’ll explore how BAs and product professionals collaborate to refine backlogs, manage value delivery, and engage stakeholders.

Topics Include:

  • The Agile Mindset for BAs

  • Product ownership horizons (strategy, initiative, delivery)

  • Backlog refinement and prioritization techniques

  • Engaging stakeholders in iterative value delivery

  • Aligning POA practices with Agile business analysis

Who Should Attend:
Perfect for analysts working in Agile environments, product owners, or those looking to bridge BA and product ownership competencies.
