Business Analysis Certification Study Tips & Strategies

Oct 31, 2024 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time  

BA Certification Study Tips & Strategies
Our East Tennessee IIBA Study Session for October is "BA Certification Study Tips & Strategies" so please make plans to join us. This is a great opportunity to review the steps for preparing for IIBA Business Analyst certification examines, specifically ECBA, CCBA, and CBAP.

If you are thinking about getting certified in Business Analysis, or are already planning to take an exam, please join us for this informative session.

The session is via Zoom (information below) on Thursday, October 31st from Noon to 1PM Eastern time.
