## How the Case Study Works

TrailBlaze Adventures is an outdoor adventure travel company with an 18% booking decline. The study group acts as the BA team across 5 sessions — each session reveals more context and asks them to apply the new concepts to what they now know.

**Context is gated.** The group only sees what is appropriate per session. `TrailBlaze_MasterContext.md` contains all 5 reveals — use it to know exactly what to share and when.

**Session B adds friction, not content.** An internal SME co-facilitates a 60-minute deep dive using the same case. No new ECBA theory — just real-world pushback on the work the group already did.

**The homework loop feeds every session.** Session A ends with a homework assignment. The next Session A opens with a 10–15 min share-out of that homework before any new content. Session B ends with a stretch question that also feeds into the next Session A share-out.
