## What to Look For

**In the plan (`ECBA_CaseStudy_Plan.md`):**
Does the session structure, domain sequence, and story arc match your expectations for the series? The plan is the contract for Sessions 2–5.

**In the slides (`Session1_Slides.md`):**
Does the Session A format work for your room and facilitation model? The run-of-show table and per-slide timing are the template every future session will follow.

**In the handout (`Workshop_Handout.md`):**
Is this the right exercise depth for your audience? The participant experience in Session 1 sets expectations for the full series.

**In the practice questions (`Session1_PracticeQuestions.md`):**
Are the MCQs at the right difficulty level? Do the rationale explanations support how you want ECBA concepts taught?

**In the style guide (`ECBA_Style_Guide.md`):**
Does the voice, tone, and brand alignment represent the chapter correctly? Any changes here apply to all 24 existing documents and everything built in Sessions 2–5.

---

## The Ask

One of three responses:

1. **Proceed** — build Sessions 2–5 using Session 1 as the template.
2. **Proceed with revisions** — note the changes needed; build continues after alignment.
3. **Hold** — note what needs to be resolved before the build continues.
